Assessment Information

The assessment for this part of the course comprises of two elements. Both elements will have in-class support time dedicated to the assessment to allow for formative feedback to be provided to students.

Overall Assessment Weighting (25% of 60 credits)

Part 1: EDP Method Section 12.5%

Part 2: PsychoPy Experiment 12.5%

Detailed Requirements

Part 1: EDP Method Section (12.5%)

To comprise of a Design, Materials & Procedure Section of a Method section submitted as a Word Document, in the format of a method section of a psychological journal (600 words maximum). It should be written in a scientific style and specify:

Design

  • the type of study design you are proposing to use
  • IVs and Levels
  • DVs

Materials

Describe the parameters that you set to control your stimuli in the experiment to allow a reader to replicate your study. This should include how you selected your simuli for each condition. See the Stimuli section of the starter reference for an example.

Procedure

Describe how a participant would run the task in a manner that allows a reader to replicate your study. See the Procedure section of the starter reference for an example.

References (APA Style)

  • In-text Citations
  • A reference Section

Part 2: PsychoPy Builder Experiment (12.5%)

  • .psyexp format with any associated files
  • Participant instructions
  • Practice block with 8 trials, with feedback
  • Trial block with 36 trials which must collect accuracy and RT data
  • Debrief
