How will this help me for my assessment?

You should be in an excellent position to work on your experiment. You have already submitted your Method section, you have a trial visualisation to help you understand how a single trial will run. You should have all of your stimuli ready, so now it's a case of taking all the knowledge from the course, and applying it to your own work.
